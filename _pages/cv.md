## Selected Research Projects

### Qìfield – Externalizing Qi as a Palmar Haptic Field

XR Tai Chi system combining:

- palm-centric multimodal haptics (airflow + vibration)
- gesture recognition
- EMG-gated feedback

Built with Unity → serial JSON → ESP32 stack to render directional “Qi flow”.

---

### EMG-Driven Pseudo-Haptic Feedback for VR Surgical Training

VR training system using surface EMG to drive effort-adaptive pseudo-haptic feedback.

The system modulates visual, audio, and vibrotactile cues when muscular effort deviates from target levels.

---

### Spider-EMS Interaction System

Dual-hand web-shooting framework combining:

- LineRenderer interaction logic
- Raycast collision detection
- EMG intent sensing
- EMS actuation feedback

Used to simulate traction and impact sensations in XR.

---

### Brain Body Jockey (B2J)

BMI-driven robotic interaction system.

Implemented EEG signal acquisition → inference → robotic arm control pipeline on Raspberry Pi OS.

Demonstrated at **Expo 2025 Osaka (Japan Sports Agency)**.
